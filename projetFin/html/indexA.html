Internationally

Going abroad

Each year, many students have the option of completing a semester of study during their second year of DUT or during their year of professional license. For this, students must apply during the year preceding their departure.
SHOW MORE

Come study at IUT

At the IUT in Lens, all the courses are given in French. A certain level of understanding of the French language is required. The IUT offers diplomas in 4 streams accessible to foreign students either to come and study in training on an individual basis, or as part of an agreement signed with a partner establishment of the IUT for targeted training. These sectors correspond to commerce, management, IT, but also digital media.
SHOW MORE

Internship proposal

During their second year of DUT or professional license, it is possible for everyone to do an internship whether in France or abroad. Each company can allow our students to be able to train in a field corresponding to them. For this, it is possible to offer an internship using the form below.
SHOW MORE

Advice

Adapting to a new country can sometimes be complicated for everyone. This is why the IUT of Lens offers you various advices so that your stay goes in the best possible way. You will find both advice on finding accommodation, successfully adapting to the new country, as well as help with preparing administrative documents and much more.
